Attitude is Everything! Attitude is Everything is a book based on Jeff Keller's journey of being a motivational speaker. Being a lawyer, he was doing well, but something was amiss. So he decided to make a gradual transition and start working as a full-time motivational orator in 1992. Attitude Is Everything, is divided into 3 Parts. The first part, Success Begins in The Mind, is about how your beliefs in mind reflect your attitude & your life can change by changing your thoughts. In the second part, Watch Your Words, you will learn how your attitude is reflected in your words & how positive language can bring you closer to your goals. Part three, Heaven Helps those who Act is about action steps you need to take to turn your dreams into reality.
